## SOP-004:ShipGeneralItem
---

##### Owner:  
##### Status: Draft  
##### Document Control:

---

### Purpose:
- To define the standardized procedure for packaging and shipping general (non-ceramic) eBay items.
- To ensure items are protected during transit.
- To reduce shipping damage, returns, and buyer disputes.

This procedure applies after:
- An item is sold on eBay

---

### Pre-Shipment Verification

__ Confirm correct item using SKU  
__ Verify item matches listing photos and description  
__ Inspect for damage prior to packaging  
__ Confirm accessories or included parts (if applicable)  

---

### Packaging Requirements

__ Select appropriate box size  
__ Allow 2–3 inches of space on all sides for padding  
__ Do not use oversized box unnecessarily  
__ Remove or cover any old shipping labels  

---

### Internal Protection

__ Wrap item in protective material (bubble wrap, foam, or paper)  
__ Protect corners and fragile areas  
__ Fill all empty space inside box with paper - do not pack paper tighly . let it   
__ Ensure no internal movement when shaken lightly  

For heavy items:
__ Reinforce bottom of box with additional tape  
__ Double-box if needed  

For electronic items:
__ Protect screens or displays  
__ Use anti-static bag if applicable  

---

### Sealing Procedure

__ Use proper packing tape (no masking or painter’s tape)  
__ Tape all seams  
__ Reinforce bottom and top edges  
__ Do not leave gaps  

---

### Labeling

__ Apply shipping label flat and fully adhered  
__ Remove or fully cover previous labels  
__ Add “Fragile” label if applicable  

---

### Final Shipping Check

__ Correct item in box  
__ SKU verified  
__ No movement inside box  
__ Box fully sealed  
__ Label correct and legible  
__ Tracking number uploaded to eBay  

---

### Rules & Constraints

- No item ships without SKU verification.
- No direct item-to-box contact without padding.
- No empty space inside the box.
- No reused damaged boxes.
- No shipping before order verification.
