### Purpose:
- To define the standardized procedure for packaging and shipping eBay ceramic shipping.
- To ensure ceramics are protected from breakage during transit.
- To reduce shipping damage and returns.

This procedure applies after:
- Sold Cermaic on eBay 
---

### Pre-Packaging Inspection

__ Clean and dry ceramic(s)  
__ Inspect for chips or cracks  
__ Confirm correct SKU before packaging (if applicable)
__ Confirm OD and Lenghth are with in tolerance and ID if sold by ID

---

### Box Selection

__ Select appropriate box  
__ Standard box: 4x4x36 (cut height down if needed)

### Standard Wrapping Procedure

__ Wrap ceramic(s) in clean paper and secure with blue tape  
__ Apply 2 even layers of bubble wrap  
__ Secure bubble wrap with blue tape

__ Cut wood or durable material support same length as corner piece  
__ Ensure support width fits snugly inside box  
__ Lay ceramic into corner piece  
__ Use support board to close open side of box
__ fill empty areas with paper or flexible foam 

---

### ðŸ”¹ If Ceramic is 70mm OD to 90mm OD:

__ Cut PVC or heavy-duty cardboard tube 4 inches longer than ceramic  
__ Cut wood support same length as tube  
__ Ensure width fits inside box  
__ Wrap ceramic in paper  
__ Apply one layer of bubble wrap and secure with tape  
__ Insert into tubing  
__ Use 4x4x4 cardboard scrap pieces as end caps  
__ Secure tubing with tape  

---

### Final Packaging Check

__ Ceramic is centered inside box  
__ No movement when shaken lightly  
__ Reinforcement secure  
__ Box properly taped  
__ Shipping label applied  

---

### Rules & Constraints

- No ceramic ships without reinforcement appropriate to its OD size.
- No empty space inside box.
- No direct ceramic-to-box contact.
- No reused damaged packaging materials.
