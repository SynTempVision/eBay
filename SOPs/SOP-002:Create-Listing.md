### Purpose:
- To define the standardized process for creating accurate, complete, and professional eBay listings.
- To ensure listings are consistent, traceable, and aligned with SynTemp Vision inventory control procedures.
- To reduce listing errors, returns, and approval rework.

This SOP applies to:
- Identified items to be listed on eBay
---

### Definitions

##### Draft Listing:
- A completed but unpublished eBay listing pending internal approval.

##### Listing Approval:
- Internal confirmation required before publishing a listing live on eBay.

##### Custom SKU:
- Internal SynTemp identifier used for traceability and inventory control.

üîπ **Step 1: Photography Requirements**

- **Remove or obscure:**
    - Old auction stickers
    - University labels
    - Lab property tags
    - Any source-identifying labels
 
- **Image Quality:**
    - Proper lighting
    - No heavy shadows
    - No excessive empty space
    - Background clean and consistent
    - Crop and straighten images as needed

- **First Photo (Primary Image):**
    - Entire item shown (Arial Photo)
    - Powered ON (if applicable)
    - All included parts/components visible

- **Aditional Photos: Required Angles:**
    - Front
    - Back
    - Sides
    - Top

- **Close-ups of:**
    - Damage
    - Missing components
    - Wear and tear
    - Dents or blemishes

- **Identification Photos:**
    - Model number
    - Serial number (if allowed)
    - Manufacturer label

- **Special Category: Burnishing Tools & Boring Bars**
    - Must be photographed on green cutting mat
    - Include:
        - Overall length
        - Diameter
        - NPT size
        - Shaft size
        - Attachment model numbers

---

üîπ **Step 2: Title Creation**

- Title must include (when applicable):
    - Brand
    - Part name
    - Model number(s)
    - Part number
    - Manufacturer date or expiration date (if relevant)
    - Key specifications (if applicable):
        - Length
        - Diameter
        - NPT size
        - Shaft size
        - Attachments or accessory names

---

üîπ **Step 3: Custom SKU**

- Enter SKU in eBay **Custom Label** field.
- SKU must appear in at least one listing photo.
- SKU must match InvenTree record.
- SKU must match physical tag on item.

---

üîπ **Step 4: Item Category**

- If using ‚ÄúHave one to sell? Sell now‚Äù:
    - Verify auto-selected category is correct.
- Otherwise:
    - Manually select most accurate category.
- Category must reflect primary function of item.

---

üîπ **Step 5: Item Condition**

- **Used**
    - Use standard verbiage:
      > Pulled from a working environment. Item has some usual wear-and-tear scuffs and blemishes but is overall in good condition. See pictures for more detail.

- **New**
    - Include ‚ÄúNEW‚Äù in title if space allows.
    - Specify unopened or sealed status in description if applicable.

- Condition must match photo evidence.

---

üîπ **Step 6: Description Structure**

- Item title in **BOLD**
- Include:
    - Size
    - Dimensions (inside dimensions if applicable)
    - Length(s)
    - Model number
    - Part number
    - Compatible systems or units (if known)
    - Any relevant technical specifications

- Required eBay verbiage:
  > CHECK OUT OUR OTHER ITEMS....EVERYTHING MUST GO!!! http://stores.ebay.com/BOOMERS-SURPLUS

- Do not exaggerate functionality.
- Do not include unsupported claims.

---

üîπ **Step 7: Shipping Setup**

- **Weight < 1 lb:** USPS  
- **Weight > 1 lb:** UPS  
- **Weight ‚â• 150 lbs:** Freight or Local Pickup  

- Must include:
    - Accurate weight
    - Accurate dimensions
    - Padded for proper shipping material estimate
        - weight plus 5 plbs
        - Length / width / heighth plus 3-5 inches
    - No guessing

---

üîπ **Step 8: Quality Check**

Before saving draft:

- Check for typos
- Verify model numbers
- Confirm SKU accuracy
- Ensure photos match description
- Confirm shipping weight/dimensions
- Ensure no source-identifying labels visible

---

üîπ **Step 9: Approval**

- Save listing as Draft.
- Submit for internal approval.
- DO NOT publish without approval.

---

##### Rules & Constraints

- No unnecessary filler words.
- No ALL CAPS except ‚ÄúNEW‚Äù when applicable.
- Maximize keyword relevance.
- No copied competitor descriptions.
- No hiding damage.
- SKU must match inventory system.
- Photos must represent actual item being sold.
